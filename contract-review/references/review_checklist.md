# Contract Review Checklists

## General Contract Review Checklist

### 1. Document Validity & Execution
- [ ] All parties correctly identified with full legal names
- [ ] Parties have legal capacity to enter into contract
- [ ] Proper authorization from authorized representatives
- [ ] All signature blocks completed
- [ ] Dates of execution present and consistent
- [ ] Witnesses/notarization if required
- [ ] Corporate seals affixed (if required in China)

### 2. Definitions & Interpretations
- [ ] All key terms properly defined
- [ ] Definitions used consistently throughout
- [ ] No circular definitions
- [ ] Interpretation clause present (if needed)
- [ ] Language priority specified (if bilingual)

### 3. Essential Commercial Terms
- [ ] Subject matter clearly described
- [ ] Price/consideration specified
- [ ] Payment terms and schedule clear
- [ ] Performance obligations detailed
- [ ] Timelines and deadlines specified
- [ ] Delivery/acceptance criteria defined

### 4. Representations & Warranties
- [ ] Scope appropriate for transaction
- [ ] Material qualifications identified
- [ ] Survival period specified
- [ ] Linked to indemnification provisions
- [ ] Knowledge qualifiers appropriate

### 5. Risk Allocation
- [ ] Limitation of liability clauses present
- [ ] Liability caps appropriate and balanced
- [ ] Indemnification obligations clear
- [ ] Insurance requirements specified
- [ ] Force majeure provision included

### 6. Termination
- [ ] Termination rights clearly stated
- [ ] Notice periods specified
- [ ] Termination for cause defined
- [ ] Consequences of termination addressed
- [ ] Survival clauses identified

### 7. Dispute Resolution
- [ ] Governing law specified
- [ ] Jurisdiction/venue identified
- [ ] Dispute resolution mechanism chosen
- [ ] Arbitration clause (if applicable) complete
- [ ] Costs allocation addressed

### 8. General Provisions
- [ ] Entire agreement clause
- [ ] Amendment procedure specified
- [ ] Assignment/transfer rights addressed
- [ ] Notices provision complete
- [ ] Severability clause included
- [ ] Counterparts clause (if needed)

## Shareholder Agreement Checklist

### Share Ownership & Structure
- [ ] Total shares and shareholding percentages
- [ ] Share classes and rights attached
- [ ] Fully diluted capitalization table
- [ ] Vesting schedules (if applicable)
- [ ] Anti-dilution provisions

### Transfer Restrictions
- [ ] General prohibition on transfers
- [ ] Pre-emptive rights (right of first refusal)
- [ ] Tag-along rights (co-sale rights)
- [ ] Drag-along rights
- [ ] Permitted transfers specified
- [ ] Transfer pricing mechanism
- [ ] Founder/key person lock-up periods

### Governance
- [ ] Board composition and appointment rights
- [ ] Board meeting procedures
- [ ] Quorum requirements
- [ ] Voting thresholds for key decisions
- [ ] Reserved matters requiring special approval
- [ ] Observer rights (if any)
- [ ] Management rights and limitations

### Information Rights
- [ ] Financial reporting requirements
- [ ] Frequency of reports (monthly/quarterly/annual)
- [ ] Audit rights
- [ ] Inspection rights
- [ ] Confidentiality of information

### Exit Mechanisms
- [ ] IPO provisions and lock-up
- [ ] Trade sale procedures
- [ ] Redemption rights
- [ ] Put options
- [ ] Call options
- [ ] Liquidation preferences
- [ ] Exit timelines

### Protective Provisions
- [ ] Non-compete obligations
- [ ] Non-solicitation clauses
- [ ] Intellectual property assignment
- [ ] Confidentiality obligations
- [ ] Key man insurance

### Deadlock Resolution
- [ ] Escalation procedures
- [ ] Mediation requirements
- [ ] Buy-sell mechanisms (shotgun clause)
- [ ] Forced sale provisions

### Good/Bad Leaver Provisions
- [ ] Definitions of good leaver vs bad leaver
- [ ] Consequences for each scenario
- [ ] Valuation methodology for shares
- [ ] Payment terms

## Employment/Service Agreement Checklist

### Basic Terms
- [ ] Parties correctly identified
- [ ] Position/title specified
- [ ] Reporting line clear
- [ ] Start date specified
- [ ] Employment type (full-time/part-time/contract)
- [ ] Probation period (if applicable)

### Compensation & Benefits
- [ ] Base salary amount and currency
- [ ] Payment frequency
- [ ] Bonus/commission structure
- [ ] Equity/stock options (if any)
- [ ] Benefits package detailed
- [ ] Expense reimbursement policy
- [ ] Social insurance contributions

### Duties & Obligations
- [ ] Job responsibilities described
- [ ] Performance expectations
- [ ] Work location
- [ ] Working hours
- [ ] Reporting obligations
- [ ] Exclusivity/non-compete during employment

### Intellectual Property
- [ ] Work product ownership clause
- [ ] Assignment of IP rights
- [ ] Moral rights waiver
- [ ] Pre-existing IP identified
- [ ] Disclosure of inventions

### Confidentiality
- [ ] Definition of confidential information
- [ ] Obligations during employment
- [ ] Post-termination obligations
- [ ] Duration of obligations
- [ ] Return of materials on termination

### Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation
- [ ] Geographic scope reasonable
- [ ] Time period reasonable
- [ ] Restricted activities defined
- [ ] Customer non-solicitation
- [ ] Employee non-solicitation
- [ ] Consideration for restrictions

### Termination
- [ ] Notice periods specified
- [ ] Termination for cause defined
- [ ] Termination without cause allowed
- [ ] Severance provisions
- [ ] Garden leave provisions (if applicable)
- [ ] Post-termination obligations

### Compliance
- [ ] Compliance with Labor Contract Law
- [ ] Working hours limits
- [ ] Overtime provisions
- [ ] Annual leave entitlement
- [ ] Sick leave provisions
- [ ] Maternity/paternity leave
- [ ] Social insurance compliance

## NDA/Confidentiality Agreement Checklist

### Scope
- [ ] Parties clearly identified
- [ ] Purpose of disclosure specified
- [ ] Definition of confidential information
- [ ] Exclusions from confidential information
- [ ] Forms of information covered

### Obligations
- [ ] Standard of care specified
- [ ] Use restrictions clear
- [ ] Disclosure restrictions clear
- [ ] Permitted disclosures identified
- [ ] Copies and derivatives addressed

### Permitted Disclosures
- [ ] Legal/regulatory requirements
- [ ] Professional advisors
- [ ] Affiliated entities
- [ ] Required notices and procedures

### Term & Termination
- [ ] Effective date
- [ ] Duration of confidentiality obligations
- [ ] Return/destruction of materials
- [ ] Survival of obligations post-termination

### Remedies
- [ ] Acknowledgment of irreparable harm
- [ ] Injunctive relief provision
- [ ] Damages not limited to injunctive relief
- [ ] Costs and attorney fees

### Additional Provisions
- [ ] No license granted
- [ ] No obligation to disclose
- [ ] No representations on information
- [ ] Relationship of parties clarified (no partnership/JV)
